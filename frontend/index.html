FAQ

Timeline Alignment FAQs

How does FutureMe generate these messages?

＋

FutureMe securely communicates with the Google Gemini API, sending details about your current goals, struggles, and desired tone. It uses robust system instruction prompts to align Gemini's contextual personality, creating an authentic, deeply reflective voice speaking directly to you from a future perspective.

Can I keep speaking with my future self?

＋

Yes! Once your timeline is aligned and your initial diagnostic letter is generated, click the "Converse with FutureMe" button. This unlocks a secure chat window where you can ask specific strategic questions about how to handle your upcoming week, your fears, or request operational feedback.

Is my reflection data secure?

＋

Absolutely. FutureMe is designed for instant secure deployment. Your personal reflection details are sent securely directly to your local backend server and passed to the Gemini API. We do not store or persist your data in any central database, keeping your journal strictly private.
